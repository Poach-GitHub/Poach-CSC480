Information Technology

Services

We provide a full range of services to the entire Contra Costa County organization. Our team:

  • Manages the central corporate computing complex
  • Manages a County Wide Area Network (WAN)
  • Manages numerous Local Area Networks (LANs)
  • Provides business and technical consulting services to departments and managers throughout the organization
